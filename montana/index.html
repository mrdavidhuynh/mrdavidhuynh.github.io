MONTANA SPRAYPAINT WEB EXPERIENCE

VISUAL DESIGN AND USER EXPERIENCE

This project was an experiential website that was meant to emphasize what makes the brand of Montana Cans spraypaint different than any other brand and to showcase why spray paint is such a unique medium for art.

THE USER EXPERIENCE

This website can be "skinned" based on various artists in the street art community that use Montana brand spray paint and spray paint accessories. Selecting the drop down menu provides thumbnails of the different selectable artists.

Based on the artist selected, different Montana products are presented that best suits that artist's style. The products are showcased through video that plays seamlessly as the user scrolls through the page

THE BRAND EXPERIENCE

These video clips are meant to show the unique aspects of spraypaint cans. The way they feel in your hand, the way you have to shake them, and their ability to cover large surfaces are all part of the brand experience that users will have when they use a can of Montana spraypaint. We wanted users to feel this while they interact with the website as well.

ARTIST GALLERIES

Large galleries based on actual finished projects done using Montana products are presented at the end of each artist's page.
